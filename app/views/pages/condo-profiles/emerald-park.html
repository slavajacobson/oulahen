Builder's Description: Distinguished by a striking architectural concept, the graceful lines and luminous green glass of Emerald Park will dramatically punctuate the monochromatic skyline of North York. Within these two towers, a lifestyle of uncompromising elegance unfolds and magnificent vistas of the lake and surrounding neighbourhoods reveal themselves. Emerald Park is a unique gem of 2 towers sitting atop a 4-storey retail and commercial podium. It is strategically located at the best corner in North York at Sheppard and Yonge. This vibrant location has direct access to the subway and will be steps away from the most dynamic shops, restaurants, theatres and clubs as well as easy access to Hwy 401. The grandeur of the suite is guaranteed to impress, with rooms that interconnect to create living spaces ideal for entertaining, along with the most elegant design features ranging from euro-influenced Kitchens, a Miele appliance package and a fabulous balcony or terrace with every suite.
