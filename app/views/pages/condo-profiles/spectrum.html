Builder's Description: Spectrum's two dramatic condominium towers comprise a total of 500 suites and are connected by a sophisticated central lobby. Its curved glass walls dominate the North York skyline from a prominent vantage point at Yonge Street and Highway 401. The sky-lit central lobby features a 24-hour Concierge to greet guests and supervise the state-of-the-art security, a dramatic span of floor-to-ceiling windows and stylish seating lounges overlook a private garden and the city skyline. Menkes' attention to fine detail comes to life in its smartly designed and exceptionally appointed Platinum Collection kitchens.
