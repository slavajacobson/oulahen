1 Avondale

Built: 2007

Builder: Shane Baghai

Floors: 20

Units: 96

Condo Corp: 1809

Property Manager: Del Property Management - 416-222-7566

 

Building Amenities: Gym, Concierge, Sauna, Hot Tub, Party Room, Guest Suites, Business Centre/Board Room.

 

Builder's Description: Created with an eye to luxury loft living as well as ‘green living,’ One Avondale Lofts is a breakthrough in condominium design. Located in uptown Toronto, just steps from the Sheppard subway line, this elegant tower features full-height condominium lofts, proving you don’t have to live downtown to experience a loft lifestyle. All suites feature floor to ceiling glass windows, terraces, gas barbecue hook-ups, imported appliances and cabinetry and millwork from Europe. Residential amenities include a well-appointed fitness spa, entertainment and media room. As the first building in Avondale to feature its own wind and solar power generators, One Avondale represents Shane Baghai’s dedication to energy conservation.
