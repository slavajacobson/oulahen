Building Amenities: Gym, Concierge, Yoga/Rec Room, Pool, Sauna, Hot Tub, Party Room, Billiards/Games Room, Business Centre/Board Room, Deck/Garden, Squash.

 

Builder's Description:  The tower has twenty-three floors with 237 spacious units ranging in size from 1240 to 1790 square feet. The building is surrounded by its own extensive and professionally maintained green space. This includes a Japanese garden with a pond and bridge and other tranquil areas where residents can relax or stroll. Lounge chairs are provided for the outdoor patio that surrounds the indoor swimming pool. A barbecue terrace is adjacent to the beautiful VIP Room which overlooks the indoor swimming pool. Both the room and terrace are available to residents for private gatherings as well as a larger party room that is accessible from the lower lobby. The impressive two-level lobby is attractively furnished and staffed with a concierge and a valet 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Both lobbies contain professionally landscaped plantings year-round and extensive amenities are also available. Unlike many condominiums today, expenses such as heat, electricity, water and digital cable TV are all included in the common element expenses. The only additional costs at Manhattan Place are property taxes and (optionally) telephone, HDTV, and Internet services. Manhattan Place has always set high standards and continues to offer outstanding value. Manhattan Place is within easy walking distance of the Centre for the Arts, North York Public library, two large shopping centres each of which includes a multi-movie complex, the Yonge and Sheppard subways, a wide variety of restaurants, stores and services plus five parks. Highway 401 is less than five minutes away.
