View Listings at: Bayview & Sheppard

View Larger Map

600 Sheppard Avenue East
Built: 2011

Builder: Daniels

Floors: 6

Units: 159

 

Building Amenities: Gym, Concierge, Yoga/Rec Room, Party Room, Billiards/Games Room, Deck/Garden, Theatre.

 

Builder's Description: MERCI Le Condominium is the newest residence to be added to Daneils' landmark NY Towers Community at Bayview and Sheppard. MERCI will combine authentic Parisian-inspired architecture with modern amenities and superior appointments to offer a sophisticated lifestyle opportunity in the heart of uptown Toronto. This boutique-style residence is resplendent in the classical touches of an ornate Parisian design marked by elegance of a traditional mansard roof and charming dormer windowns. The overall effect will fit as seamlessly into the urban landscape as it would on a street in Paris.
