Builder's Description: Both of the Waldorf condominium buildings were completed by the Daniel’s Group in 2003 with 230 suites-20 floors in the East Tower and 221 suites-20 floors in the West Tower. Part of the New York Towers development at Sheppard and Bayview. 2 Rean and 8 Rean Drive have 1 bedroom, 2 bedroom or 2+1 bedroom layouts with one or two bathrooms respectively. The Waldorf towered condominiums have great access to nearby Bayview village, 401 highway, subway, Loblaw’s, YMCA and schools. The condominiums boast high quality of finishes and style that the Daniels group has been known for. You can calm and relax yourself in a charming and peaceful environment through various activities provided in the exercise room, swimming pool, sauna, library, virtual golf, billiards, ping-pong, outdoor BBQ patio, and a multi-purpose recreational room.
